About

Schroder European Real Estate Investment Trust plc is a United Kingdom-based closed-ended investment company. The Company is engaged in property investment in the Continental Europe. The Company's objective is to target a dividend yield of 5.5% based on the euro equivalent of the issue price at launch. The Company's objective is to provide a regular and attractive level of income together with the potential for long-term income and capital growth. The Company's properties are located in Paris, Berlin, Stuttgart and Hamburg. The Company intends to invest in a portfolio of institutional grade income-producing properties with low vacancy and creditworthy tenants. It will invest principally in the office, retail, logistics and light industrial property sectors. It may also invest in other sectors, including, but not limited to, leisure, residential, healthcare, hotels and student accommodation. Schroder Real Estate Investment Management Limited is the Company's investment manager.
