About

Mountview Estates P.L.C. is a property trading company. The Company owns and acquires tenanted residential property throughout the United Kingdom and sells such property when it becomes vacant. The Company operates through two segments: Property trading and Property investment. The Company's portfolio of categories of properties includes regulated tenanted (residential) units, assured tenancy units, ground rent units and life tenancy units. The Company's rental income is derived from approximately five sources, which include regulated tenancies, assured tenancies, assured shorthold tenancies, life tenancies and ground rents. The Company also deals in the acquisition of life tenancies. In the interim, the maintenance of these properties is usually the responsibility of the life tenant. All the Company's properties are located in Belsize Park, London. Its subsidiaries include Hurstway Investment Company Limited, Louise Goodwin Limited and A.L.G. Properties Limited.
